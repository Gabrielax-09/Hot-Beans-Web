Our History

Hot Beans Web was set up in 2010 by Christina Falou because of his passion for web development. She decided to create a business after she couldnt find any places to get recruited. Raciscm unfortunatley made the search for job really hard for her. After many sad and discriminating experiences she decided to create her own business with no discrimination. The orgin of the name HOT Beans is a funny story. When Miss Falou was thinking of creating her own business and plan all the stages. She got really hungry and craved Hot Beans. While eating Hot Beans she continued planning the business. Due to the delicius Beans , she made great decisions that have affected her and all the members of Hot Beans Web. Now in 2023 Hot Beans Web works for several companies and provides the best experience possible. We are always looking to grow so check out the job openings to see if you can join the Hot Beans Web family.
